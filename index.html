In the game of 2048, players slide numbered tiles on a 4x4 grid to combine identical numbers, with the ultimate goal of reaching the tile labeled "2048." The rules are simple: players make moves in four directions using their respective arrow keys on keyboard—up, down, left, or right—each turn causing all tiles to slide as far as possible in the chosen direction. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into a new tile with a value equal to the sum of their original values. New tiles with a value of 2 or 4 appear after each move. The game continues until the player is unable to make any more moves, leading to a conclusion. Strategic planning and careful positioning are key to achieving the highest score in this engaging puzzle game.
